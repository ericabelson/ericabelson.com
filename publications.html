Popular & Professional Writing

Medium Articles

  • Finding Earth Engine Data Doesn’t Have to Be a Struggle: Let AI Agents Do the Heavy Lifting – Co-authored with Kristopher Overholt (Google), this post explains how agentic AI built with the Agent Development Kit (ADK) can simplify the search for datasets in Google Earth Engine by translating research questions into dataset recommendations.
  • Building an Agentic Scientific Research Team with Google’s Agent Development Kit – Co-authored with Kristopher Overholt (Google), this post walks through creating a multi-agent research team using ADK to automate scientific literature review and methodology extraction.

LinkedIn Posts

  • Preparing National Science Foundation (NSF) proposals is time-consuming… – Introduces author-affiliations, a lightweight Python tool that automates the creation of NSF COA documents by pulling structured author and affiliation metadata from Crossref and OpenAlex, based on ORCID export lists.
  • AI and Academic Writing: Ethical Integrity – A reflection on GenAI’s role in academic publishing, proposing the inclusion of AI prompt logs to support ethical transparency, foster trust, and help reduce barriers for non-native English speakers and researchers with disabilities.
  • AI and Accessibility in Academia: A Step Toward Inclusion – A post exploring how GenAI technologies like LLMs and vectorization can reduce barriers for disabled and non-native English-speaking researchers, enhancing accessibility and inclusion across academia.
  • AI and Mitigating Bias in Academia – Reflections on how GenAI can both introduce and mitigate bias in academic writing, with a focus on promoting inclusion, accessibility, and diverse representation through the responsible use of AI tools like LLMs.
